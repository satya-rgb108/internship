About us

SpaceX

SpaceX was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars. SpaceX manufactures the Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy launch vehicles, several rocket engines, Cargo Dragon, crew spacecraft, and Starlink communications satellites. “On The Border By The Sea And Beyond!” is the new slogan. Brownsville has fallen for SpaceX in a major way and you can see it everywhere in the city.
